GUIDELINES FOR PMCF STAFF

1. The borrower will utilize the loan only for the purpose declared in loan application form. LUC must be conducted within 60 days of loan disbursement.

2. All financial transactions will be held only in the center meeting at a central designated place.

3. A Member has the option to repay the loan in Weekly/Fortnightly/Monthly mode. There will be a moratorium for the loan repayment equivalent to the period of one instalment span selected by the borrower in Loan application.

4. The tenure of loans will be as chosen by the member in the loan application.

5. There is no penalty for prepayment of loans but penal charges can be taken in case of delayed payments. No security deposit or margin will be collected from the borrower by the lender.

6. PMCF will acknowledge every financial transaction in the presence of members by making entries into their loan cards.

7. Pre-closure of the loan will be done by updating both the promissory note and loan card.

8. Loan application forms will not be returned to the members as it is the property of the organization.

9. The borrower will be charged an amount equivalent to 1% of the loan amount as a non-refundable Loan Processing Fee (exclusive of applicable tax) and applicable insurance premium towards Credit Shield Insurance at the time of disbursement of each loan.

10. The pricing of loans involves only three components, viz. Interest Amount, Loan Processing Fees and applicable Insurance Premium.

11. The borrower agrees to pay a Life Insurance Premium of   ₹ {{LPC|floatformat:0}}    for a sum assured equal to the disbursed loan amount. The life insurance cover term shall be equal to the loan term & any pre-closure of the loan will get the insurance cover terminated.

12. In the event of any eventuality of self and/or Co-Insurer's death giving rise to a claim under the Group Insurance Scheme, the claim proceeds should be utilized to liquidate the outstanding loan amount of PARISHKAR MICROCARE FOUNDATION. I authorize PARISHKAR MICROCARE FOUNDATION to receive the outstanding loan amount of the claim proceeds payable on the happening of the contingent event covered under this policy from the Insurance partner. Any residual amount, shall be paid by the Insurer to me or my nominee/beneficiary as the case may be.

13. Member/s have an option to change their nomination for the life insurance cover any time during the cover term.

14. PMCF will not hold any type of asset as collateral/ security deposit/ margin money against any loan in the Joint Liability Group (JLG) model.

15. PMCF is authorized to share the client details to Credit Bureau agencies approved by Regulator/Government Authorities. PMCF gives an assurance that the privacy of borrower data shall be respected.

16. All changes in the terms & conditions of the Loan, Interest rates and other charges of loan will be only prospective.

17. The loan is required for the purpose mentioned in the loan application form.

18. Warranty and after-sales services are provided by the manufacturer and PMCF will not be responsible for any lapse on the part of the manufacturer.

19. Customers should apply for loans/product loans on their own wish, PMCF does not force anyone to borrow/take a loan or buy a product.

20. In addition to the amount specified in the loan card, members are not required to pay any kind of cash or any other form to the employees or any other person.

21. PMCF maintains transparency of all the norms and conditions with the borrower and communicates to the members in the local language or in the language, they understand.

22. Grant of loan is not linked to any other product/services offered by the company or third party.

23. PMCF does not discriminate against customers based on ethnicity, age (Subject to IRDA norms), sexual orientation, political affiliation, caste, creed, or religion.

24. An Insurance claim may be repudiated under conditions such as Death date not falling within Insurance tenure; Submitting incorrect or unclear claim document details; Inactive or dormant bank account details; Issuance of Death certificate by an unauthorized agency.

25. Borrower shall be classified as NPA (Non-Performing Asset), if any of her loan facility is in overdue for more than 90 days. NPA status of the loan shall change only when the entire arrear i.e. principal and interest is paid by the borrower. Asset classification shall be done borrower-wise and not facility - wise. It is also applicable for Restructured loan (if any).

26. Loan facility shall be classified as SMA 0 when it is in overdue up to 30 days; SMA-1 when it is more than 30 days and up to 60 days; SMA-2 when it is more than 60 days and up to 90 days and classifies as NPA when it is more than 90 days.

27. PMCF as a regulated entity will be responsible & accountable
